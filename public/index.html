How It Works

Simple. Fast. Fair.

Whether you're a smallholder farmer in Kiambu or a restaurant owner in Westlands, FarmAd makes it easy to trade fresh Kenyan produce at transparent prices — with M-Pesa payments built in.

📋
List Your Produce

Post available produce with photos, quantities, and prices in minutes. No middlemen, no complicated forms — just your farm, your terms.

🔍
Buyers Discover & Connect

Supermarkets, restaurants, and individuals browse listings by county, crop type, or price. Message farmers directly through the platform.

💚
Transact with Confidence

Complete deals through FarmAd's secure M-Pesa integrated system. Ratings and verified profiles ensure trust on both sides of every transaction.
